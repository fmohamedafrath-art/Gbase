About Us

With over a decade of experience, Gbase Technologies has become the leading computer repair and customization service in the region.

Our Mission & Vision

At Gbase Technologies, we believe that technology should work for you, not against you. Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient, and affordable computer solutions for both individuals and businesses.

We specialize in laptop and desktop repairs, software troubleshooting, Windows installation, and custom gaming PC builds. Our team of certified technicians stays up-to-date with the latest technology trends to deliver the best solutions.

We pride ourselves on our quick turnaround times, transparent pricing, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you need a simple software update or a complete custom PC build, we've got you covered.
